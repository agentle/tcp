22. of December, aſwell within the Citie of LONDON and the Liberties thereof, as in other
Pariſhes in the skirtes of the Cittie, and out of the Freedome, adioyning to the
Citie: According to the report made to the Kings moſt excellent Maieſtie,
by the Company of Pariſh Clearks of the ſame
CITIE.
