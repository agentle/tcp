
      
         
            
            
            
               Lord William Cecill and mannars Baron of Roos laufull heyre of the Baronies of Burghley and Earldome of Excetor, Ambassader Extraordinary for the Sacred and Royall Maieſtie of the moſt illuſtrious Kinge of great Brittaine to his very louing Brother the moſt illuſtrious Catholique Kinge of Spaine.
            
               Anno .1616.
