                  ❧Palmer.
               
                  NOwe god be here who kepeth this place
               Now by my fayth I crye you mercy
               Of reaſon I muſt ſew for grace

               Wherof your pardon axt and wonne

               To tell thys whiche ſhalbe begonne
               In order as may come beſte in myndy
               I am a palmer as ye ſe
               Whiche of my lyfe much part hath ſpent
               In many a fayre and farre countre
               As pylgrymes do of good intent
               At Hieruſalem haue I bene
               Before Chryſtes bleſſed ſepulture

               A holy place ye may be ſure

               Many a ſalt tere dyde I ſwete

               And gone the ſtacions all arow
               Saynt peters ſhryne and many mo

               Hath taken hede and marked muche

               And rounde about to amyas
               At ſaynt Toncomber and ſaynt Tronion
               At ſaynt Bothulph and ſaynt Anne of Buckſton
               On the hylles of Armony where I ſee Noes arke
