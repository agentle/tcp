
      
         
            
            
            ¶ A Proclamation for the ſpeedy payment of the Queenes Rents, Tenthes, and other dueties.
            
               
                  ELIZABETH Dei gratia Angliae, Franciae & Hiberniae Regina, fidei defenſor, &c.
               Maiori & Vicecomit. Ciuitatis London Salutem. Praecipimus vobis, quod ſtatim viſis praeſentibus in ſingulis locis infra ciuitatem praedictam, tam infra libertates, quam extra, vbi magis expedire videritis ex parte noſtra publicas Proclamationes fieri fac. in haec verba.
            THe Queenes Maieſtie meaning to vſe no extremitie towardes her Tenants, although in ſome part ſhee might vpon occaſion already giuen, hath thought meete to giue knowledge to all her Farmors and Tenants within the Citie of London, which ought to haue paide their Rents, Tenthes, and other dueties at Michelmas laſt, or at the furtheſt before this time vpon ſundry great paines by the order of her Lawes, and haue not, that they ſhall make payment to the Collectour, or Receiuer generall of the ſaide Citie, or into the receipt of her Exchequer, within twelue dayes after this Proclamation. And if any perſon ſhall herein make default, at this time, or hereafter, her Maieſtie muſt giue order to extend the Tenour of her Lawes therefore prouided as reaſon is.
            ¶ God ſaue the Queene.
Et hoc ſub periculo incumbenti nullatenus omittatis. T. Me ipſa apud Weſtm. 21. die Decembris. Anno regni noſtri quinto.
               
               Marten.
Peripſam Reginam. &c.
            
         
      
      
         
            Imprinted by Richarde Iugge, and Iohn Cawood, Printers to the Queenes Maieſtie.
