               Of this mooſt cryſten kynge in vs it lyeth not
               His tyme paſſed honour ſuffycyent to prayſe
               But yet though that / that thyng envalue we may not
               Our prayers of ſuertye he ſhall haue alwayes
               And though that atropoſe hathe ended his dayes
               His name and fame ſhall euer be dred
               As fer as phebus ſpredes his golden rayes
               Though henry the ſeuenth alas alas lyeth dede
            
            
               But nowe what remedye he is vncouerable
