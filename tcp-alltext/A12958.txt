
      
         
            
            
            Steven Steple to maſt Camell▪
            
            
               REſt ye mery vayre zyr, I trow ye be maſte Camell.
               ichā on meſſage to you zent, vrom goodmā Geffrai Chapel
               A hate ye zent a bottell a hay, and bad commaunde ma tye ye,
               And vorde dyng he ſpake ye to, a preyd ye dat ye wood hy ye,
               And zuch kynd daintrels as a had, zum draf he hate ye zent,
               A zyerd ye vor to gnab deron, vor der is no better in Rent,
               Tis goode and zoote & alzo new. to mende your zyckyſh brayne
               Vor weele a zeete ye ha great ſtud, & zet your ſelf to payne,
               Him vraid yer wits wud zore ye vayle, ye gyn to raue zo zone,
               A dynkte ye wyll be mad, al out, beuore dat May be done:
               And moch he merueilt dat ye wud, zo chorlſhly to vin wryghte,
               And dat ye zent vm ſuch an Anſer, dat zounded noding right
               To Harry Hoball zyr ye wrot, as pearit in your letter,
               zwap yer ſpeckles vp ſe naſe, and looke about ye better,
               And Anſer Geffray Chappell zyr, dat toke ye de zupplicacion,
               Vor his name is not Hary whobal, ich zwear by gods zauacion
               How zay ye now yore ſpeckles be on, can ye vorſtande his byll,
               ych ween a trete ye (zereuerence a you) to do Churchard no yl
               And her cha brought yor byll ayen, corrupt it or ich go,
               Vor vende gods vorbod man I zedge, to let it go vorth zo:
               But well ich zee yor brayne is dicke, yor wits be curſtly vext,
               Prey God ye be not zyde yor zelf, er be to morow next:
               Deruore go couch and ſleap a nowe, and dan com to yor parte,
               And dyte a wyſer dyng dan dat, or all is not wort a vart.
            
            
               Now ych ha myne arnde a do,
               Chud ha ye yor head to heede a
               And ve good maiſter Churchard to,
               And zo God be yor ſprede a.
            
         
      
      
         
            Imprinted by Richard Lant.
