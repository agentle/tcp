            Newly corrected, with Additions of dyuers neceſſarie Preſidents, meete for al ſuch as deſire to learne the fourme and maner how to make all maner of Euidences, and Inſtruments, as in the Table of this Booke more plainlie appeareth.
            Anno Domini 1588.
            
               Jmprinted at London by Rychard Tottle.
