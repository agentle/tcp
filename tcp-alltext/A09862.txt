
      
         
            
            
            A right ſtrange and woonderful example of the handie vvorke
of a mightie God, to mooue vs wretched ſinners to amendement
of our wicked lyues, by this lamentable Spectacle for al men & women
to behold, of the birth of three children borne in the pariſh of PASKEWET, in the County of
Monmouth, on Thurſday, the third of February laſt. 1585. And are are as this preſent
 to be ſeene at London.

                  
                     
                        •
                     awde
               
               
                  Ioane Io
