               ¶ And note well yt in rent ſek it behouyth for hym that ſewith for the rent ſek for to ſhew a dede to the tenaunt or ellꝭ ye tenāt ſhall not be chargyd of ye rent but wher the rent ſek was rent ſeruyce before / as in this caſe / lord meſne & tenant & euery of them holdyth of other by homage fealte &. x. s. of rēt / ye lord ꝑamount purchayſith ye lādꝭ or tenementꝭ of ye tenant / all the ſignioury of the meſne but ye rent is extynet / And for this cauſe this rent is become rent ſek / & ye rent ſeruyce chaūgyd / for he may not diſtreyn for this rent / & i this caſe he ye demaū dith the rēt ſhal neuer be chargyd to ſhew a dede / alſo in a wryt of mordaunceſtour ayle nor beſayle of rent ſek it nedyth not to ſhew a ſpecyalte for y• y• theſe wryttꝭ of poſſeſſiō do cōprehend a tytle within them ſelff that is to ſay that the aunceſtour was ſeiſed of the ſame rent and continuyd his poſſeſſiō by cauſe of which ſeyſyn the law ſuppoſith that it is alſo auerable by the countrey tamen quere for ſome ſuppoſe that yt be houyth of neſſite to ſhew forth a dede for that that rent ſek is a thyng agayns comyn ryght as well as rent charge / But in aſſiſe of nouell diſſeyſyn and in a wryt of entre ſur diſſeyſyn brought of rent ſek it behouyth of neceſſite to ſhew forth a dede for vat that rent ſek is a thyng agayns comyn ryght except in ye caſe befor ſeyd where it was rent ſeruyce before
               
                  
And aſſiſe of nouell diſſyeſyn a wryt or entre ſur ceſſaunt conteyn with in them no tytle but ſuppoſe a diſſeiſyn to be don to the playntyff and of the intēdement of the law the diſſeyſyn gyffyth no cauſe of auerment agayns comyn ryght but of neceſſite it be houyth to ſhew forth a dede
               Sewte ſeruyce is to come to the court of thre wekys to. iii. wekes by the hole yere & for that a man ſhalbe diſtreynyd and not amercyed.
               Sewte reall is to come to the court of the lete / and that is not but. ii. tymys in the yere and for that a man ſhalbe amercyed and not dyſtraynyd.
